About Me

My name is Mohamed Alaa, I am a Myp5 student in notion international school in Giza. This website is a part of an awareness campaign I’m working on, that revolves around the problems that face children online and how to ensure their safety. So, I searched for answers from reputable and trusted sources to create the perfect guide for parents to understand what they’re children are facing and what problems you should care about the most. In each segment I talk about the problem’s prevalence, it’s effects and I provide solutions on how can you help protect your children and prevent the occurrence of this problem from the beginning.
